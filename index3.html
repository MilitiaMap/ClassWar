About the Map

Our interactive map allows you to explore all 58 counties of California. Click on any county to see its name and hover to highlight its boundaries.

The map uses OpenStreetMap data and the Leaflet.js library to provide a smooth, interactive experience on any device.

Did You Know?

California is the most populous state in the United States with over 39 million residents.

San Bernardino County is the largest county by area in California (and the contiguous United States), covering over 20,000 square miles.

Alpine County is the least populous county in California.

California Geography

California features diverse geography, including:

  • The Sierra Nevada mountain range
  • The Central Valley, one of the world's most productive agricultural regions
  • Over 800 miles of Pacific coastline
  • The Mojave and Colorado deserts
  • Ancient redwood forests
